Government Polytechnic is a Government funded institution situated at Turuvekere,Tumakuru District in a sprawling campus of 5 acres. It was established in the year 2007-08. The Polytechnic is wholly run by the Government of Karnataka and comes under the purview of Directorate of Technical Education. This is a newly started institution and is a recipient of various projects like State Government Finances, Women's Hostel from MHRD. Established in the year 2007-08 offering affordable quality education to rural students inculcating Technology, Virtues and Knowledge to meet the needs of the industry standards.
